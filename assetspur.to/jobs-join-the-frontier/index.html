JOIN THE FRONTIER OF ASSETSPUR DeFi

We’re on a mission to discover the possibilities of finance in the blockchain and cryptocurrency world. We believe in the potential of DeFi Decentralized Finance.

Since 2017 we have grown to a team of more than 40. We’re currently profitable, processing billions of dollars worth of crypto, and still growing. As a ASSETSPUR team member, you will have the opportunity to ride a revolutionary wave, grow in your role, gain valuable experience that will be indispensable in the future of financial technology.

CAREERS AT ASSETSPUR

SINGAPORE - IT Helpdesk Specialist - Full-Time Onsite

Responsibilities:
-Maintain computers and peripheral equipment
-Responsible for server and network maintenance
-Hardware and software support

Requirements:
-Responsibility, good collaboration, humility, and ability to deliver dependently
-Computer or related major is preferred
-Have a strong sense of security
-Familiar with the installation and troubleshooting of the existing mainstream system, understand the basic technology of the network, and be familiar with the working principle of the TCP/IP protocol
-Familiar with the construction and usage of common servers

Conditions
-Be a part of the future of DeFi finance technology with ASSETSPUR
-Fast moving, challenging and unique business problems
-International work environment and flat organisation
-Competitive salary
-Flexible working hours, Casual work attire

SINGAPORE - Front-End Web Developer - Full-Time Onsite

-Optimize web applications to maximize speed and scale
-Build libraries and frameworks that support complex web applications, speedup development efficiency and improve code quality

Requirements:
-Above 5+ years developing production JavaScript code and strong knowledge of React.JS.
-Distributed/remote/global-team working experience.
-Your active Github/NPM portfolio.
-Familiarity with the whole web stack, including protocols and server side techniques.
-Familiar with Server-Side Rendering.
-Familiar with the source code of one or two famous front-end libraries.
-Knowledge of TypeScript.

Conditions
-Be a part of the future of DeFi finance technology with ASSETSPUR
-Fast moving, challenging and unique business problems

-Competitive salary
-Flexible working hours, Casual work attire

HONG KONG - Finance Manager - Full-Time Remote

Responsibilities
-Maintain accounting books and prepare monthly reports to Global operations team
-Prepare monthly, quarterly, & annual financial statements in accordance with local accounting GAAP
-Work together with tax consultants to prepare and submit tax returns on time
-Review local contracts initiated by local office in particular on the payment terms
-Assess tax implication of the contracts
-Work with operation and compliance team to ensure the compliance of local rules and regulations
-Document & compile necessary reporting whilst liaising with external auditors
-Build & maintain banking relations

Requirements:
-Bachelor degree in accounting/finance/economic/business administration) is a must
-Good knowledge of local accounting GAAP, Certified Public Accountant is a plus
-More than 10 years of experience at relevant positions
-Familiar with local laws and regulations
-Experienced and knowledgeable of accounting software, payroll procedures, and management systems

Conditions
-Be a part of the future of DeFi finance technology with ASSETSPUR
-Fast moving, challenging and unique business problems
-International work environment and flat organisation
-Competitive salary
-Flexible working hours, Casual work attire

CAYMAN ISLANDS - Office Adminstrator - Full-Time Remote

Responsibilities
-Assist and support in the initial set up of our local organisation from a very early stage.
-Provide administrative support for all BUs including managing office suppliers, catering, meeting scheduling, travel bookings, courier logistics and other operational tasks.
-Coordination of employee events and activities, including annual dinner, team buildings, anniversary celebrations.
-Support office renovation and relocation as well as premise maintenance.
-Enhance and optimise corporate policies and proceedings.
-Manage budget planning, expense vendor management, purchasing and fixed asset management.
-Coordinate with Global HR and Admin teams to implement global strategies and tailor them to local needs.
-Partner with and support tasks from the local regional director.
-Be the on-the-ground expert for local knowledge and current affairs.

Requirements:
-Minimum 5 to 7+ years experience in office management or administration.
-Fluent English. Knowledge of French are beneficial.
-Experienced with policy design, communication and process improvement.
-Prior experience in procurement or HR is a plus.
-Well organised, accountable with an attention to detail, hardworking and a quick learner.
-Can-do attitude, open-minded and approachable personality for understanding and supporting employees’ needs.
-Ability to handle multi-tasks under pressure.

Conditions
